Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Libris ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using Libris, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Profile picture (optional)

2.2 Book Data

When you use the App, we collect:

  • Book titles, authors, and genres you add
  • Reading status (to-read, reading, completed)
  • Ratings and personal notes
  • Reading dates and goals
  • Book cover images you upload

2.3 Usage Data

We may automatically collect:

  • App usage patterns and features accessed
  • Device type and operating system
  • Error logs for debugging purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Sync your book library across devices
  • Generate AI-powered book recommendations
  • Analyze your reading habits (when you request it)
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important service-related notifications

4. AI Features and Data Processing

Libris uses Google's Gemini AI to provide book recommendations and reading habit analysis. When you use these features:

  • Your book list data is sent to Google's AI services
  • This data is processed to generate personalized recommendations
  • We do not store AI conversation history
  • Google's AI services have their own privacy policies

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted backend-as-a-service provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Row-level security policies
  • Regular security audits

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our App (Supabase, Google AI)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of users

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Export: Download your book library as a CSV file
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Disable AI features if you prefer not to use them

8. Children's Privacy

Libris is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
